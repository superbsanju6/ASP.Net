Thinkgate Policies

 

Password Reset

In the case of needing a password reset (i.e, becoming locked out of the system, forgotten password) you will need to contact the primary point of contact within your district in order to get the password reset. Thinkgate support cannot reset individual user’s password due to security reasons.

 

Unproofing Tests

Once a test has been proofed it cannot be unproofed. All changes must be made to a test before it is proofed.  The only way to make changes to the proofed test is to copy the test and make changes to the copy of the test then proof the copied test.

 

Warning: Once a test had been copied it receives a new test ID, therefore bubble sheets printed for the original test will not work for the copied test.  New bubble sheets will need to be printed for the copied test.

 

Who can contact Thinkgate support?

The predetermined primary points of contact within your district are the only users that can contact Thinkgate Support. Please contact your district's Elements™ administrator in regards to who can contact Thinkgate support within your district.

 

If I have an enhancement idea or find a bug in the system, whom do I tell?

If you have a good idea for an enhancement to the system or have details on any bug that you find you will need to contact one of the primary points of contact within your district.  The primary point of contact can then directly contact Thinkgate support with the reported bug or enhancement request.  Thinkgate support will then submit the enhancement request or reported bug to the Thinkgate developers for review and prioritization.
