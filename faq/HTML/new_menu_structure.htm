New Menu Structure

 

Where are the Find and Add menu options?

The Find and Add option that were selectable text in the old system have now been converted to buttons located to the right of the specific topic.  The Find option has been replaced with a magnifying glass image and the Add button with a green "plus" sign.  The Administer District test option has also been replaced with the Dashboard image next to District and Classroom tests.

 

What changed in the menu structure, what menus moved?

With the new menu structure a few menu options have been moved to more appropriate areas.  Items, addendums and images have all been moved to the Assessment tab under the Item Builder menu.

 

Curriculum plans have been broken into two parts, the pacing guide and the assessment blueprint.  The pacing guide is located in the Curriculum tab under the Curriculum Planning menu (if purchased by your district).  The Assessment Blueprint can be found in the Assessment tab under the District Blueprint menu (if purchased by your district).

 

All other information regarding the new changes to the system can be located in the Summer Release notes located in the Reference Center.
